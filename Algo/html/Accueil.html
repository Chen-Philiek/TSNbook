Project

Our project endeavors to create a Tailored Social Network (TSN) platform, introducing advanced features beyond conventional social media platforms such as Facebook.

The platform will be constructed upon a graph-based structure, drawing inspiration from the FOAF (Friend of a Friend) Ontology (refer to Fig. 1). This architecture will empower users to establish connections, engage in interactions, and share content in a manner that is both personalized and intuitive.
